# Girl unDead

This is a revision for 1.18 of an add-on campaign Girl unDead for the strategy game Battle for Wesnoth.

## Campaign Description

Malica, a young girl who died unexpectedly, has become undead due to incomplete revival, but somehow Wesnoth has been trying to capture her. Meanwhile, Cyclos, a young elf, is searching for his shamans, who have been taken away by orcs; the two, originally opposing races, begin to confront the mighty Kingdom together, Malica to learn the true meaning of their plot, and Cyclos to save his people.

(Intermediate level, 10 scenarios.)
